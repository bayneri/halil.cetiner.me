<p>
if I could, I would<br>
no regrets, whatsoever<br>
<br>
apples should remain red<br>
green apples, be green<br>
<br>
birds chirp<br>
ducks quack<br>
<br>
what makes a duck a bird<br>
is neither chirp<br>
nor quack<br>
<br>
clocks tick<br>
bells ring<br>
<br>
what makes an hour an hour<br>
is neither tick<br>
nor ring<br>
<br>
names differ<br>
things remain<br>
<br>
what makes a thing itself<br>
is neither name<br>
nor shape<br>
<br>
water flows<br>
why wouldn’t words?<br>
<br>
everything does,<br>
Heraclitus said<br>
<br>
you know nothing, Jon Snow<br>
nothing can be known<br>
</p>
